Growing Bright Futures, One Angel At A Time

At Angel Home Family Child Care, we provide a safe, loving, and stimulating environment where little angels shine!

Welcome

Angel Home Family Childcare is the perfect place for young children to reach their potential. Located in Lynnwood, WA, our licensed daydcare center offers a balance of structured learning, creative exploration, and outdoor fun. Our certified teachers understand the importance of creating a safe and nurturing environment. We focus on each child's specific needs and development to prepare them for success in school and life. With healthy meals and engaging activities, we're here to make early learning joyful for families in Lynnwood and surrounding neighborhoods.

  • Safe and Clean Environment
  • Experienced Educators
  • Play-based Learning
  • Healthy and Balanced Meals
  • Flexible Scheduling
  • Enrichment Activities

Discover Our Programs

Infants (< 12 months)

Gentle, attentive care for your baby in a safe and nurturing environment. We focus on bonding, sensory play, and supporting early milestones.

Toddlers (1 - 3 years)

Hands-on learning through play, music, and movement. Our toddler program buildds language, motor skills, and social connections.

Preschool (3 - 5 years)

A balanced mix of structured learning and creative play to prepare your child for kindergarten and beyond. Early STEM, art, and literacy activities included.
