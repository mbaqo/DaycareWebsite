Infant Program

Learn about our Infant Care in Lynnwood, WA

We provide a safe, nurturing space where your baby can thrive during their most important devlopment stage. Our licensed infant program in Lynnwood, Washington is designed for babies less than 12 months old. In this stage, we prioritize bonding, sensory exploration, and building trust.

We follow each baby's individual schedule for feeding, naps, and play, ensuring a smooth transition from home to daycare. We support early milestones such as tummy time, rolling, reaching, and first words through gentle, interactive activities.

Toddler Program

Fun learning for busy toddlers in Lynnwood

Our toddler daycare program in Lynnwood is all about curiosity, movement, and discovery. Designed for children ages 1 to 3, this program balances play-based learning with gentle structure to encourage independence, communication, and problem-solving skills.

Toddlers in our care enjoy music and movement, sensory play, early art projects, and outdoor exploration in a safe, supervised environment. Our teachers focus on social skills such as sharing, cooperation, and self-expression while introducing early literacy and number concepts.

Preschool Program

Preschool Readiness and Kindergarten Preparation in Lynnwood

Our preschool program in Lynnwood prepares children ages 3 to 5 for a confident start in kindergarten. We combine early literacy, math, and science activities with art, music, and imaginative play to keep learning fun and engaging.

Our curriculum includes early STEM projects, pre-writing skills, story time, and group activities that build confidence, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities. We encourage curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning, setting the foundation for lifelong academic success.
