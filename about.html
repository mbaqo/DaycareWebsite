About Us - Angel Home Family Child Care

At Angel Home Family Child Care, we know that choosing the right daycare is one of the most important decisions you’ll ever make for your child. You want a place where they are safe, loved, and inspired to learn and that’s exactly what we provide. Located in the heart of Lynnwood, Washington, our licensed childcare center has been a trusted choice for families seeking high-quality early childhood education in a warm, home-like environment.

Our Story

Angel Home Family Child Care began with a simple belief: every child deserves a safe, loving space to grow, explore, and discover their unique potential. What started as a passion for caring for children has grown into a thriving, trusted daycare serving families in Lynnwood and surrounding neighborhoods. We combine the comfort and personal attention of a home-based setting with the structure and professionalism of a licensed childcare center. Our goal is to create an environment that feels like a second home where parents feel confident and children feel excited to come each day.

Our Mission

Our mission is to nurture the whole child socially, emotionally, physically, and academically through play-based learning, positive guidance, and strong relationships. We focus on helping children develop not only the skills they need for school, but also the confidence, curiosity, and kindness that will help them thrive in life.

Visit Our Facility

We invite you to tour the child care and meet our child care director, Huma. Schedule your visit today and let us show you how we make early learning joyful, safe, and inspiring for every child.
